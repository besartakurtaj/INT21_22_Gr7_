About

From our humble beginnings until now.

2009-2011
Our Humble Beginnings

We started this journey more than a decade ago with just a basic blog where we wrote tips and advices on how to improve mental health and just health in general.

May 2011
A Website is Born

A couple years after starting we had the idea of offering more than just advice therefore creating a whole new website where we could give all of you lots more resources on self-help and self-love.

December 2018
Transition to Full Service

With the evolution of technology now being able to implement a lot more things into our website we made it more exclusive including everything from self-help books and podcasts to inspirational videos and online meetings.

July 2020
Social Media Infiltration

Summer of last year we opened accounts on all social media sites to keep up with the newest trends and to keep in touch with all of you. Now you can follow our accounts anywhere and interact with us.
